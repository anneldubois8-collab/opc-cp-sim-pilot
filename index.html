CP Prosthetic Simulation – Pilot

Welcome to the Transtibial Pilot Case

This tool is modeled on the ABC CP Written Simulation exam and focuses on clinical decision-making for an older dysvascular transtibial amputee receiving a first definitive prosthesis.

You will see a series of possible actions at each stage of care. For each action, choose whether you would perform it in this situation. After each choice, you'll see feedback explaining whether the action is considered Essential, Appropriate, Low value, Inappropriate, or Contraindicated, with enrichment commentary providing extra biomechanical / prosthetic detail.

This is a study aid only. It is not an official ABC exam and is not intended to predict your exam score.
