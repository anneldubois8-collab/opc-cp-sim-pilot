CP Prosthetic Simulation – Pilot

Welcome to the Transtibial Pilot Case

This interactive case models the clinical reasoning required for the ABC Certified Prosthetist Written Simulation exam.

You will be presented with a real-world patient scenario and must decide which clinical actions you would and would not take. Feedback will appear after each choice explaining whether your decision was appropriate and why.

This tool is for study purposes only and is not affiliated with ABC.
